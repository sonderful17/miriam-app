## Privacy Commitment

Your reproductive health data is sensitive. Miriam is designed with privacy at its core:

- **No backend servers**: All data stored in browser localStorage
- **No cloud sync**: Your data stays on your device, always
- **No accounts required**: No login, no tracking, no analytics
- **No data collection**: We literally cannot see your data
- **Open source**: Full transparency in how your data is handled

**Trade-off**: You must use the same device to access your data. There is no cross-device sync by design.
